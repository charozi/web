FAQ: Why Do You Need to Verify Your Account?

Why do I need to verify my account?
To prevent bots, spam, and trolls from disrupting the server.

How do I verify my account?
Follow the verification steps in the designated channel.

What happens if I don’t verify?
You won’t get full access to the server.

Is verification safe?
Yes! It’s just to confirm you’re a real user.

I’m having trouble verifying. What should I do?
Contact a moderator for help.
